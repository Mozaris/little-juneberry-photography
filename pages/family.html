Tips for Your Family Session

Color Coordination

Choose a color palette with 2-3 complementary colors. Avoid matching exactly - coordinating looks more natural.

Plan for Little Ones

Schedule sessions around nap times and bring snacks. Young children often do best in the morning when they're fresh.

Best Timing

Golden hour (one hour before sunset) provides the most flattering light for family portraits.

Focus on Connection

The best family photos capture genuine love and connection. Don't worry about perfection - focus on enjoying time together.

Tips for Your Session

Coordinated Outfits

Choose colors that complement each other rather than exact matches. Avoid busy patterns and logos.

Meaningful Locations

Consider places that are special to your relationship - where you met, got engaged, or love to spend time together.

Golden Hour

The best lighting occurs during the "golden hour" - about an hour before sunset for warm, romantic lighting.

Be Yourselves

Relax and have fun! The best photos happen when you're comfortable and enjoying each other's company.

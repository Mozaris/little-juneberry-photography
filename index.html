Hello & welcome

Weddings that feel like you: warm, honest & unforgettable

I'm Allie, the heart behind Little Juneberry Photography. I believe the best wedding photos aren't posed to perfection. They're the in-between moments: the happy tears, the belly laughs, the quiet glance across the room.

From the Duluth lakeshore to a backyard in Superior, I photograph your day as it truly unfolds so that years from now, one look at your gallery brings the whole feeling rushing back.

Frequently asked questions

I'm based in northwest Wisconsin, just south of the Twin Ports, and I photograph weddings all over Duluth, Superior, and across Minnesota and Wisconsin. Travel beyond the region is absolutely welcome; just mention your location when you reach out.
Wedding collections start at $1,500 for six hours of coverage and go up to $2,500 for a full day with an album. Every package includes a complimentary engagement session and a private online gallery, and can be customized to fit your day.

The story

Hi, I'm Allie

Little Juneberry Photography

Welcome, I'm so glad you're here.

I'm Allie, a wife, a proud mom to two little girls, and the heart behind Little Juneberry Photography. Home for us is Solon Springs in northwest Wisconsin, and I photograph weddings all over the Twin Ports of Duluth and Superior and well beyond. Life at our house is busy and full, with two daughters, two lovable dogs, and a whole lot of laughter, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

Sewing has always been one of my favorite ways to slow down and create, and I think that's exactly why I fell for photography. Both are about taking little pieces of a moment and stitching them into something you'll treasure for years. With a wedding, those pieces are the held breath before the doors open, the squeeze of a hand during vows, the way you both can't stop smiling on the dance floor.

My approach

Relaxed, real, and all about you

My goal is for you to forget I'm even there, to be so wrapped up in your day that the camera disappears and what's left is just you, your person, and the people you love most.

I photograph weddings the way I'd want my own remembered: warm, honest, and unhurried. Expect gentle guidance when you want it, plenty of room to simply be together, and a friendly face who'll happily round up the wedding party, fix a crooked boutonnière, and find the prettiest light. Your finished gallery comes back warm and true to color, edited to feel timeless instead of trendy, so it looks just as lovely in twenty years as it does today.
